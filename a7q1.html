Industrialized Al comes under the fourth industrial revolution. The emerging trends in technology and environment advances in a very close interaction between physical, digital and biological worlds. This digital transformation with the use of Al will result in short term boost of the market as it will requires people to research and study the market and develop a bot that is able to provide the necessary and reliable output with the limited resources in our environment. A majority of jobs are at risk with the growing use of AI in different industries, such as salespeople, accountants, real estate agents, and insurance agents to drivers. In short, the jobs that required unskilled labour are at risk of being eliminated first. On the other hand, this use of artificial intelligence has the potential to vastly increase employment and a transformed mechanized of production stage. The growth of AI can also lead to emergence of new professions, new job descriptions, new sectors, new initiatives and new business opportunities. In fact, a recent report by the most prominent IT industry research firm Gartner estimates that enterprise technology powered by AI will create more jobs than it eliminates. In other words, AI cannot create on its own, it needs a human touch to lead and direct the action that will further help in revolutionize the market. Moreover, the industries use AI mainly to do a specific task on repeat rather that work on a task that require an emotional intelligence and an experience of the human world to be successful.
